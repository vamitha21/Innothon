INNOCOM 26

INNOCOM 26 is our platform for students to incubate and express their talents. It fosters entrepreneurship and innovation, guiding students towards a successful and independent future. Our club aims to elevate the standards of our students, creating achievers and trendsetters in the field of computer science.

INNOTHON 26

INNOTHON 26 is a 30-hour hackathon where students are challenged to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. Problem statements of InnoThon 24 are provided by our industry partners, giving students the opportunity to tackle genuine challenges faced by industries today.
